Hello! I'm Mine, a Product Manager based in Cork, Ireland.

I recently concluded nearly 7 years as a Senior Product Manager at Trendyol, Turkey's largest e-commerce company and partner of Alibaba Group. With a $16 billion valuation, Trendyol operates in 41 countries and 15 different languages.

I specialize in SEO, growth, and AI/LLM automation projects. At Trendyol, I managed the product development processes of three technical teams within the technology department, working alongside more than 2,000 developers and 200 product managers.

My expertise includes product positioning, stakeholder management, agile methodology, A/B testing, and strategic planning. I'm particularly passionate about leveraging AI and machine learning to optimize user experiences and drive business growth.

I hold a Master's degree in Engineering Management with a thesis on e-commerce product recommendation systems, exploring the effects of Generative AI and NLP methods on customer purchasing behavior.
